1. what is transfer learning 
        Transfer learning is a machine learning technique where knowledge gained from solving one problem is reused to improve performance on a different but related problem.  Instead of training a model from scratch, practitioners leverage a pre-trained model that has already learned general patterns from a large dataset, allowing the new model to achieve high accuracy with less data and significantly reduced computational costs ( we use a pretrained model related model to reduce the time and cost with work on the model)                   The source and target tasks are different, often involving feature extraction from a large dataset for a specific new task


2. Word embedding
        word embeddings are the “what” (vector representations of words), while Word2Vec is one of the “hows” (an algorithm to generate those vectors). They are related but not identical concepts. Word embeddings can come from Word2Vec or other embedding methods, and choosing the method depends on your application, corpus size, and desired properties of the vectors
